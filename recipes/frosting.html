Butter Cream Frosting

A sweet and creamy frosting for your sugar cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. box of powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 stick salted butter (softened)

Steps

  1. Beat with electric mixer 2 minutes scraping bowl often
  2. Add food dye coloring of your choice.
  3. For thinner consistency, add milk a little at a time.
